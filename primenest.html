FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines depend on scope. A 5-page website takes 1–2 weeks. A web application with backend typically takes 4–8 weeks. Enterprise systems and SaaS platforms are scoped individually — usually 3–6 months. You receive a detailed timeline before any work begins.
Do you support M-Pesa and local payment methods?
Yes — M-Pesa Daraja API integration (STK Push, C2B, B2C, paybill, till) is one of our core specialties. We also integrate Stripe, PayPal, Flutterwave, and any other payment gateway your business needs.
What happens after the project is delivered?
Every project includes post-launch support (30–90 days depending on plan). After that, we offer retainer packages for ongoing maintenance, feature development, and monitoring. You own all source code outright.
Can you work with our existing systems?
Absolutely. We regularly integrate with existing ERP systems, CRMs, accounting software, and databases. During discovery we audit your current stack and design solutions that connect cleanly with what you already have.
Do you offer equity or deferred payment arrangements?
For qualifying startups with strong traction, we occasionally consider deferred payment or equity arrangements. We handle this on a case-by-case basis — contact us with your project details and we'll discuss the right structure.
Who owns the code and IP after delivery?
You do. Upon final payment, full ownership of all source code, design files, and intellectual property transfers to you. We sign a clear IP assignment agreement at project start.
